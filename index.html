How to find your Moxfield deck URL
  1. Open your deck on moxfield.com
  2. Set visibility to Public — Edit Deck → Visibility → Public
  3. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar
  4. It will look like:
    https://www.moxfield.com/decks/MkwJl0PJMEK0C8w_TZVhgw
  5. The highlighted part is the deck ID — you can paste the full URL or just the ID
